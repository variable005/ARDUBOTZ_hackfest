Allowances

Company Provided Allowances

Allowance Name Description Amount/Percentage Eligibility Criteria Taxability
House Rent Allowance (HRA) To help employees with accommodation expenses. ₹ 10,000 per month or 40% of basic salary (whichever is lower, as per policy) All confirmed employees residing in rented accommodation. Partially Taxable
Travel Allowance (TA) To cover expenses incurred during official travel. ₹ 200 per day for local travel; Actuals for outstation travel (as per policy) Employees on official duty requiring travel. Generally Non-Taxable (up to certain limits)
Dearness Allowance (DA) To mitigate the impact of inflation. 15% of basic salary (subject to change based on government regulations) All confirmed employees. Fully Taxable
Medical Allowance To help with medical expenses. ₹ 2,000 per month All confirmed employees. Taxable
Leave Travel Allowance (LTA) To support employees' travel during leave periods. Reimbursement of actual travel cost for self and family (twice in a block of four years, as per policy) Confirmed employees who have completed one year of service. Non-Taxable (subject to certain conditions)
